Ultimo Bar

Ultimo Bar is a roguelike deck-builder inspired by traditional Italian playing cards, and it is the first game we’ve unearthed from our archives and are currently recreating.

We are working to provide you with the most accurate reconstruction possible of the game, which, according to our findings, was released in 2003.

We have been unable to find any other traces of this game outside of our archives.

You can already play an early prototype that includes the match gameplay.
The prototype does not represent the final product: many game modes and features are still missing and temporary art and bugs may still be present.
